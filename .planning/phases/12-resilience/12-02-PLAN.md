<objective>
Wire the MQTT disconnect/connect events into the MQTT_DISCONNECTED_AT atomic, completing the RESIL-02 feedback path.

Purpose: Plan 01 put the read side (wifi_supervisor checks MQTT_DISCONNECTED_AT) in place. This plan puts the write side in place: the MQTT callback stamps the atomic on disconnect and clears it on reconnect. Without this, MQTT_DISCONNECTED_AT stays 0 permanently and RESIL-02 never fires.

Output: src/mqtt.rs with two atomic write operations added to the callback closure — one in EventPayload::Disconnected and one in EventPayload::Connected. No other files changed.
</objective>


CRITICAL CONSTRAINT from mqtt.rs module doc comment:
  "The callback MUST NOT call any EspMqttClient methods: the C MQTT task holds its
   internal mutex during event dispatch and any re-entrant call will deadlock.
   Atomic stores and SyncSender::try_send are safe from within the callback."

Both MQTT_DISCONNECTED_AT.store() and .compare_exchange() are non-blocking atomic
operations — safe to call inside the callback.
